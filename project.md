# Excel Dashboard: E-Commerce Business Analysis

## Project Overview

This project is an Excel-based interactive dashboard for analyzing an e-commerce business dataset. The dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance, order trends, customer behavior, product category performance, sales channels, and geographic performance.

The main goal of this project is to transform raw e-commerce transaction data into meaningful business insights that can support management decisions in sales, marketing, operations, and customer targeting.

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Monthly Sales and Order Trend

The dashboard shows monthly sales and order trends. March generated the highest sales, followed by February, April, and January. This indicates strong sales performance during the early months of the year.

However, sales and orders showed a declining trend from September to December. This may require further investigation and stronger marketing campaigns during the year-end period.

---

### 2. Gender-wise Sales Analysis

Female customers contributed the highest share of total sales, representing approximately 64% of overall sales. Male customers contributed around 36%.

This indicates that female customers are the primary customer segment for this business. Future marketing campaigns, product recommendations, and promotional offers can be designed with a stronger focus on female customers.

---

### 3. Order Status Analysis

Most of the orders were successfully delivered.

| Order Status | Share |
|---|---:|
| Delivered | 92.25% |
| Returned | 3.37% |
| Cancelled | 2.72% |
| Refunded | 1.67% |

The high delivery rate indicates strong operational performance. However, returned, cancelled, and refunded orders should be analyzed further to identify possible issues such as product quality, delivery delays, wrong product delivery, or customer dissatisfaction.

---

### 4. Channel-wise Sales Performance

Pickaboo generated the highest sales among all sales channels. PriyoShop and AjkerDeal also performed strongly.

Top-performing channels include:

- Pickaboo
- PriyoShop
- AjkerDeal
- Daraz
- Chaldal

This analysis helps identify which platforms are contributing most to revenue and where future promotional activities should be prioritized.

---

### 5. District-wise Sales Performance

Chittagong was the top-performing district in terms of sales. Other strong-performing districts include Patuakhali, Brahmanbaria, Bandarban, and Barisal.

This geographic insight can help the business improve regional marketing, inventory planning, and delivery operations.

---

### 6. Age Group Analysis

Millennials placed the highest number of orders, followed by Gen X customers. Boomers contributed the lowest order share.

This suggests that the business should focus more on Millennials and Gen X customers when designing digital marketing campaigns, product offers, and communication strategies.

---

### 7. Product Category Analysis

Home Appliance was the highest-performing product category, followed by Sports and Health Care.

Top categories:

- Home Appliance
- Sports
- Health Care
- Toys
- Fashion & Beauty
- Electronics

Home Appliance appears to be the strongest revenue-generating category. On the other hand, Fashion & Beauty and Electronics showed comparatively lower sales, which may need further business review.

## Business Recommendations

Based on the dashboard insights, the following recommendations can be made:

1. Focus marketing campaigns on female customers, as they generate the highest sales contribution.
2. Improve year-end sales performance through seasonal campaigns, discounts, and promotional offers.
3. Prioritize high-performing channels such as Pickaboo, PriyoShop, and AjkerDeal.
4. Investigate the reasons behind returned, cancelled, and refunded orders.
5. Increase inventory and promotional focus on Home Appliance, Sports, and Health Care categories.
6. Use district-wise insights to improve regional targeting and delivery planning.
7. Develop customer retention strategies for Millennials and Gen X customers.

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear and practical overview of e-commerce business performance. It helps identify key sales trends, customer segments, product performance, order status, and channel contribution.

The project demonstrates how Microsoft Excel can be used as a business intelligence tool to analyze e-commerce data and generate actionable insights for decision-making.
